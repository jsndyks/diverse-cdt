PROPORTION
do the *PROPORTIONAL* variations of the marks
reflect the numbers they encode?
DETECTION
can you *DETECT* individual items and see
how their visual characteristics vary?
ASSEMBLY
can you visually *ASSEMBLE* multiple
items into groups to see and relate patterns?
DISCRIMINATION
can you *DISCRIMINATE* categories of
items through their visual characteristics?
ESTIMATION
can you *ESTIMATE* quantities from items?
(Quickly, consistently and accurately?)
DENSITY
is the graphic *DATA DENSE* ?
(Many items arranged in a small space?)
SCALE
can the graphic be read at *MULTIPLE SCALES* ?
(For close and distant reading?)
COMPARISON
is the graphic *MULTIVARIATE* so relationships
between variables can be compared ?
PROXIMITY
are things that have similar 
characteristics *CLOSE* together?|(2D position, continuous)
ALIGNMENT
are the things that we may need to compare 
 *LINED-UP* ?
VARIATION
do encodings reflect the *VARIATION* in the data?
(Level of measurement, structure, identity, quantity)
CONGRUENCE
do encodings reflect the *PHENOMENA* ?
(Are they indicative of the referents?)
STRUCTURE
is any *STRUCTURE* in the data
captured in the encodings?|(Periodicity, aggregation, continuity, discontinuity)
CONNECTION
do *CONNECTIONS* show the most important
relationships between items ?
CONTAINMENT
do *CONTAINERS* show the most important
hierarchical relationships between items ?
CONSISTENCY
is hue used *CONSISTENTLY* across the
graphic to show relationships between items?
EFFICIENTY
is any *DOUBLE ENCODING* 
necessary & justified for when used for emphasis?
ANNOTATION
are visually differentiable *LABELS* 
used to identify and annotate important features?
LEGIBILITY
is all text *READABLE* with little effort?
(Succinct, horizontal, legible, complete - not truncated)
REDUNDANCY
are *NON-DATA ITEMS* minimised ?
(Subtle, in the background and not repeated?)
CONCURRENCY
can I see everything *CONCURRENTLY*?
(No scroll bars! We need to see all of the data!)

how their visual characteristics vary?
ASSEMBLY

items into groups to see patterns?
DISCRIMINATION
can you *DISCRIMINATE* categories of
items through their visual characteristics?
ESTIMATION
can you *ESTIMATE* quantities from items?
quickly, consistently and accurately
DENSITY

SCALE

close reading, distant reading
COMPARISON
is the graphic *MULTIVARIATE* so relationships
between variables can be compared ?
PROXIMITY

*CLOSE* together? (2D position, continuous)
ALIGNMENT

CONGRUENCE

periodicity, aggregation , continuity, discontinuity
